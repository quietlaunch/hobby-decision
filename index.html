FAQ

What if I disagree with the quiz output?

Use it as a starting point, not gospel. The quiz is a framework to clarify your thinking, not a mandate. Trust your gut if it conflicts.

This seems too simple for a big decision.

Simplicity is the point. Overcomplexity = paralysis. Most hobby decisions don't need 50-page analysis. They need clarity and permission.

Will this help me actually commit?

Yes. Permission (scripts) + clarity removes shame. Once you know your decision is sound, you stop sabotaging yourself with guilt.

What are the permission scripts?

Pre-written paragraphs you can use to explain your decision to yourself and others. Keep: "This is worth my time because..." Quit: "I can let this go guilt-free because..." Pause: "I'm taking a break to..."
